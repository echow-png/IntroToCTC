pterosaur art

Pterosaurs don't all have to be silly. They can be cool too.
I have six main pterosaurs that have appeared through multiple full illustrations. These are typically rendered fully, with varying degrees of realism.

Each represents one aspect of memory:

  • keeper of laws (the Head keeper)
  • keeper of knowledge
  • keeper of spaces
  • keeper of wealth
  • keeper of fear
  • keeper of connection

I use them to explore wider, abstract concepts that my other characters do not address.
